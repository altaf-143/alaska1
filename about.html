About Us

Welcome to ALASKA COMPUTER COLLEGE

Welcome to Alaska Computer College, where excellence in education meets innovation and technology. Our mission is to provide top-notch education in the field of computer science and information technology, equipping our students with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in the rapidly evolving tech industry

Founded in 2006, Alaska Computer College has grown from a small institution into a leading center for computer education in the region. Our journey began with a vision to create a premier institution that would address the growing need for skilled IT professionals. Over the years, we have expanded our programs, facilities, and partnerships to provide students with the best educational experience possible.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission


At Alaska Computer College, our mission is to provide world-class education in computer science and information technology. We aim to develop skilled professionals who can innovate and lead in a rapidly evolving technological landscape. Our commitment to excellence in education is reflected in our rigorous academic programs, state-of-the-art facilities, and dedicated faculty.

Our Vision


We envision a future where Alaska Computer College is recognized globally for producing top-tier tech talent and pioneering research. We strive to be a hub of innovation, fostering an environment where students can explore their passions, develop their skills, and contribute to technological advancements.



Academic Programs

Undergraduate Programs

Bachelor of Computer Science: A comprehensive program focusing on software development etc.
Bachelor of Information Technology: Emphasizing practical skills in IT management, networking, & system administration.
Bachelor of Software Engineering: Preparing students for careers in software development, quality assurance & project management.

Postgraduate Programs

Master of Computer Science: Advanced studies in computer science with specializations in artificial intelligence, machine learning & cybersecurity.
Master of Information Systems: Aimed at professionals looking to enhance their skills in data management, IT strategy, and business analytics.
Master of Cybersecurity: Focusing on protecting information systems, ethical hacking, and risk management.

Diploma & Certificate Courses

Diploma in Web Development: Covers front-end and back-end web technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and server-side scripting.
Certificate in Network Administration: Provides foundational knowledge in managing and configuring network infrastructures.
Certificate in Data Analytics: Introduces data analysis techniques, tools, and methodologies for business intelligence.

Programing language

PYTHON , C , C++ , JAVASCRIPT, JAVA , HTML, CSS, :- Python is a versatile, high-level language known for its readability and is commonly used in web development, data science, and automation. C is a foundational low-level language ideal for system programming, while C++ builds on C with object-oriented features, making it suitable for game development and high-performance applications. JavaScript is essential for interactive web development, working on both the client and server sides. Java, a platform-independent, object-oriented language, is widely used in enterprise solutions and Android development. HTML structures web content, and CSS styles it, ensuring responsive and visually appealing designs..
